What is our mission?

To bring students together as a team in which we build a robot that can compete in a tournament of a given contest. What is this contest/tournament about? We are part of the For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology (FIRST) organization. In this organization, we are a part of the First Robotics Competition (FRC) league. You can also view the manual for this years competition here. To view the competition for this year, play the YouTube video below.

How do we go about building our robot?

With the help of our sponsors/mentors (Local Industry, Piscataway Board of Education, PHS Robotics Alumni, and parents), we build and program our robot from scratch. Parts are given to us from FRC and we are sponsored by the Piscataway Board of Education. We spend a few weeks planning and prototyping, and the rest of the time building our robot, as well as coding, making improvements along the way.
